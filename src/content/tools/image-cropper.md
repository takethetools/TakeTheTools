### What Is Image Cropping?

Image cropping means selecting a specific region of a photo and removing everything outside it. This is used to:

- **Improve composition** by removing distracting backgrounds
- **Meet platform requirements** for profile pictures, banners, or thumbnails
- **Focus attention** on the most important part of an image
- **Reduce file size** by trimming unnecessary pixels

### Common Use Cases

**Social Media:** Instagram requires square (1:1) or portrait (4:5) images. Our tool lets you crop to these ratios instantly.

### Tips for Better Crops

1. **Rule of Thirds** — Position your subject at the intersections of a 3×3 grid for more natural-looking crops
2. **Headroom** — Leave a little space above the subject's head in portraits
3. **Check Output Size** — For web use, aim for the smallest file that still looks sharp

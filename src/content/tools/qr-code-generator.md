### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. **What is a QR Code?** A matrix (two-dimensional) barcode first designed for the automotive industry in Japan.
2. **How much data can it hold?** Up to 7,089 numeric characters or 2,953 alphanumeric characters in Version 40.
3. **What is "Error Correction"?** A mathematical technique that allows a scanner to reconstruct a QR code even if part of it is dirty or torn.
4. **Is it free to use?** Yes, the QR code technology is open-source and free for anyone to use without a license.
5. **How do I scan it?** Almost any modern smartphone camera app includes a native QR code reader.
6. **Can I put a logo in it?** Yes, by using High (H) error correction, you can replace part of the code with a logo without breaking the link.
7. **Does it expire?** No. A "Static" QR code (like the ones we generate) works as long as the underlying URL or data remains valid.

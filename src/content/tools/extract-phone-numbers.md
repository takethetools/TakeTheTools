## About the Phone Number Extractor

The **Phone Number Extractor** scans any block of text and automatically identifies and pulls out all phone numbers — regardless of format. This is a powerful tool for data cleaning, lead generation, and contact management.

### Supported Phone Number Formats

Our extractor recognizes international and domestic formats including:

- `+1 (555) 123-4567` — US International format
- `(555) 123-4567` — US Standard format
- `555-123-4567` — Dashed format
- `555.123.4567` — Dotted format
- `5551234567` — Plain digits
- `+44 20 7946 0958` — UK format
- `+92 300 1234567` — Pakistan format
- `+91 98765 43210` — India format

### Real-World Use Cases

**Lead Generation:** Scrape contact details from unstructured documents, emails, or web content and extract clean phone lists.

**Data Cleaning:** When importing contact data from PDFs, CRMs, or legacy systems, phone numbers are often embedded in messy text. Extract and reformat them automatically.

**Customer Support:** Quickly find callback numbers from long email threads or support ticket transcripts.

**HR & Recruiting:** Extract contact numbers from resumes or application forms pasted as plain text.

### Tips for Best Results

- Paste the full document or block of text — the extractor finds numbers anywhere in the content
- For international numbers, include the `+` prefix to ensure country code detection
- Review results manually if the source text contains non-phone number sequences (e.g., product codes) that may look like phone numbers

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. **What is Base64 to Image?** The process of converting a long alphanumeric string of text back into its original image format (PNG, JPG, etc.).
2. **Where do these strings come from?** Usually from databases, JSON API responses, or CSS files where images are "inlined."
3. **Is there a quality loss?** No. Base64 is a lossless encoding method. The resulting image will be a perfect bit-for-bit match of the original.
4. **How do I identify a Base64 image?** They usually start with the characters `data:image/` or a long sequence of characters ending in `==`.
5. **Does it support all image types?** Yes, as long as the string represents a valid image file (WebP, GIF, SVG, etc.).
6. **Why use Base64 instead of a file?** It allows you to store or send images alongside text in systems that don't support binary file uploads easily.
7. **Is it free?** Yes, our professional-grade tool is 100% free and works entirely in your browser.
8. **How precise is our converter?** We use the industry-standard `atob` and `URL.createObjectURL` logic to ensure your results are 100% accurate.
9. **Is Base64 good for SEO?** Only for very small icons. Large Base64 images can make your HTML/CSS files too bulky, slowing down your page performance.
10. **Is my data safe?** Yes, our tool works entirely offline in your browser; your sensitive proprietary strings never leave your computer.

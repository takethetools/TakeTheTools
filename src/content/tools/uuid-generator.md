2. **What is the difference between UUID and GUID?** GUID (Globally Unique Identifier) is Microsoft's implementation of the UUID standard. They are essentially the same.
3. **How many UUIDs are possible?** There are $2^{128}$ (roughly 340 undecillion) possible UUIDs.

7. **Does it contain my MAC address?** UUID v1 does; UUID v4 (which we prioritize) is 100% random and contains no identifying hardware data.

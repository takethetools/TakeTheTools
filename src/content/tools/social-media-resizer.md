### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. **What is a Social Media Resizer?** A digital tool that takes an original image and crops or resizes it to the specific dimensions required by different social platforms.
2. **Why can't I just use one size?** Each platform has its own layout. A rectangular Twitter header will look terrible as a square Instagram profile picture.
3. **What is the best format?** For photos, use JPG; for logos or text-heavy images, use PNG; for the fastest web performance, use WebP.
4. **What is an Aspect Ratio?** The proportional relationship between the width and height of an image (e.g., 1:1, 4:5, 16:9).
5. **How do I avoid blurry images?** Always start with a larger, high-quality image. Scaling down is safe; scaling up causes pixelation.
6. **Can I resize for LinkedIn?** Yes, we provide presets for profile photos, background headers, and post images.
7. **Is there a limit on file size?** Our browser-based tool can handle most standard high-res photography (up to 20MB).
8. **How precise is our tool?** We use the industry-standard "Canvas API Scaling" to ensure your results are 100% accurate down to the individual pixel.
9. **Is resizing good for SEO?** Yes, smaller, properly sized images improve page speed, which is a major factor for Google.
10. **Is my image safe?** Yes, our tool works entirely offline in your browser; your sensitive proprietary images never leave your computer.

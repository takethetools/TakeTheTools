### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. **How many square feet are in an acre?** Exactly 43,560 square feet.
2. **What is a Hectare?** It is a metric unit of area equal to a square with 100-meter sides (10,000 sq m).
3. **Why do farmers use Acres?** It is a deeply historical unit that relates to the work capacity of pre-industrial farm animals.
4. **How do I convert Square Meters to Square Feet in my head?** Multiply by 10 and add a small buffer (approx 7.6%).
5. **What is "Areal Density"?** It is the amount of something (like data or plants) per unit of area.
6. **Is a Square Mile larger than a Section?** No, a Section in the US PLSS is exactly one square mile (640 acres).
7. **What is the area of a football pitch?** A standard FIFA pitch is approx. 0.71 hectares or 1.76 acres.
8. **How precise is our converter?** We use high-precision floating-point arithmetic suitable for most industrial and real estate applications.
9. **Does curvature affecting area?** For very large areas (like countries), the Earth's curvature must be accounted for using "Geodetic Area."
10. **What is a "Barn"?** It is a very small unit of area used in nuclear physics (10^-28 sq m).

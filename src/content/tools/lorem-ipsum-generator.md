## What is Lorem Ipsum?

**Lorem Ipsum** is the standard "dummy text" used by the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It is loosely based on a 1st-century BC Latin text by Cicero, but its words have been altered and scrambled so they don't carry any meaningful message.

Technically, the "magic" of Lorem Ipsum is that it has a more-or-less normal distribution of letters, making it look like readable English (or other European languages) without being a distraction. If you use "Text here, text here" as a placeholder, the human brain focuses on the repetition. If you use "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet," the brain accepts it as a block of text, allowing you and your clients to focus on the font, the layout, and the overall "User Experience" (UX).

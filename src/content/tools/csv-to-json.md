### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. **What is CSV to JSON conversion?** The process of turning row-based tabular data (Comma-Separated Values) into a structured JavaScript object notation.
2. **Does it support Excel files?** You should first export your Excel sheet as a `.csv` and then paste the content into our tool.
3. **What is the "Header Row"?** The first row of your CSV which contains the names of the data categories (e.g., "ID," "Price").
4. **How do I handle nested data in CSV?** You can't directly; CSV is flat. You must convert to JSON first and then manually or programmatically add nesting logic.
5. **How big of a file can I convert?** Our tool is optimized for typical web-sized datasets (up to several thousand rows) in real-time.
6. **Can I convert back?** Yes, use our companion JSON to CSV tool for a premium data-reversal experience.
7. **What happens to "Empty Cells"?** Our tool can represent them as `null` or empty strings `""` in the resulting JSON.
8. **How precise is our converter?** We use industry-standard parsing rules to ensure your quotes, commas, and special characters are handled with professional accuracy.
9. **Is JSON better than CSV?** For machines and web apps, yes; for humans and basic spreadsheets, CSV is often easier to read.
10. **Is my data safe?** Yes, our tool works entirely offline in your browser; your sensitive CSV data stays local.

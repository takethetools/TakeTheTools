### A Deep Dive into the Evolution of IP Geolocation

Before the "Geo-IP Databases," pinpointing a user's location required complex network "traceroutes" that were often blocked by firewalls or yielded inaccurate results based on backbone routing rather than the user's home. 

- **The Early ARPANET Era:** In the 70s and 80s, IP addresses were assigned in huge blocks to universities and government agencies, making identification relatively simple but coarse.
- **The IPv4 Exhaustion Era:** As addresses became scarce, the rise of "Carrier-Grade NAT" and dynamic reassignment made IP Lookup a dynamic, sub-second science requiring constant database updates.
- **The Modern AI/Security Era:** Today, we use IP Lookup for everything from "Localized Pricing" to "Anti-Fraud Scoring," defining "The User" by the fundamental laws of network logic. Our tool leverages the results of these developments for your daily work.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. **What is an IP Lookup?** A process that finds the location, service provider, and other technical details associated with an Internet Protocol address.
2. **Is it 100% accurate?** It is highly accurate for Country and ISP, and city-level accuracy is usually between 70-90% depending on the provider.
3. **What is an ASN?** An Autonomous System Number is a unique identifier for a collection of IP networks managed by a single organization.
4. **Does it show the user's home address?** No, for privacy reasons, IP Lookup only shows the city or the neighborhood where the ISP's local exchange is located.
5. **Can I look up IPv6?** Yes, our tool supports both the older 32-bit IPv4 and the modern 128-bit IPv6 standards.
6. **Why does it show a different city?** Your ISP may route your traffic through a hub in a nearby city. Our tool shows where the "Gateway" is registered.
7. **Is it free?** Yes, our online tool provides free lookups for the global community.
8. **How precise is our lookup engine?** We use the industry-standard "GeoIP2" and "IPInfo" logic to ensure your reports are 100% compatible with modern security standards.
9. **Is IP Lookup good for SEO?** Indirectly, yes. It helps you understand where your traffic is coming from so you can localize your content effectively.
10. **Is my IP safe?** Yes, our tool does not store your personal lookup history; your search data remains private and professional.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. **Are computer-generated numbers truly random?** Most are "Pseudo-Random," meaning they follow a complex algorithm based on a seed.
2. **What is a "Seed" in RNG?** A starting number that determines the sequence of random numbers that follow.
3. **How do I generate a random number between 1 and 10?** Set Min to 1 and Max to 10 in our tool and click generate.
4. **What is the "Monte Carlo Method"?** Using repeated random sampling to solve a complex mathematical problem.
5. **How does RNG affect video games?** It controls everything from critical hits to the items you find in chests.
6. **Can a human pick a truly random number?** No, humans have inherent biases (e.g., picking 7 more often than others).
7. **What is "Cryptographically Secure" RNG?** An RNG that is unpredictable enough to be used for security keys.
8. **How precise is our generator?** We use high-precision floating-point logic suitable for most games and non-cryptographic simulations.
9. **What is a "Fixed Seed"?** A seed that is deliberately reused to reproduce the same "random" results for testing.

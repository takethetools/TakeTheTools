### How to Generate a SHA-256 Hash: Step-by-Step

Generating a SHA-256 hash is a high-fidelity, computationally intensive process.

#### The Computational Flow
1. **Initialize State:** Eight 32-bit registers (from `a` to `h`) are set to the fractional parts of the square roots of the first eight prime numbers.
2. **Apply Round Constants:** 64 specific constants (the fractional parts of the cube roots of the first 64 primes) are used to "scramble" the data.
3. **The 64 Rounds:** For every 512-bit block of your text, the algorithm performs 64 rounds of logical XOR, SHIFT, and ROTATE operations.
4. **Final Result:** The final register values are concatenated to produce the 64-character hex string.

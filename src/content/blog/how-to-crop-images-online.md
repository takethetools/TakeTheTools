Whether you need to cut out a specific part of a photo, resize for a profile picture, or create a thumbnail, our **Image Cropper** gives you precise control with a drag-and-drop interface.

## What You Can Do with the Image Cropper

- **Free-form Crop**: Draw a custom crop area.
- **Fixed Ratio Crop**: Lock to standard ratios like 1:1, 16:9, 4:3.
- **Rotation**: Rotate the image 90° or 180°.
- **Flip**: Mirror horizontally or vertically.

## How to Crop an Image

1. Open the [Image Cropper](/tools/image-cropper).
2. Upload your image (PNG, JPG, WebP).
3. Drag the crop handles to define your area.
4. Use the toolbar to adjust ratio or rotation.
5. Click **Crop** and download your result.

## Common Crop Ratios

| Ratio | Use Case |
|---|---|
| 1:1 | Instagram posts, profile photos |
| 16:9 | YouTube thumbnails, desktop wallpapers |
| 4:3 | Standard photography, presentations |
| 9:16 | Instagram/TikTok Stories |
| 2:1 | Twitter header images |

## Privacy Note

All processing happens locally in your browser using the **react-easy-crop** library. Your images are never uploaded to any server.

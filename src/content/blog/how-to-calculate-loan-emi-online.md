Whether you're planning a home loan, car loan, or personal loan, understanding your monthly payment (EMI) is crucial for financial planning. Our **free Loan Calculator** gives you instant results.

## What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment you make to repay a loan. It includes both the principal amount and the interest charged.

**EMI Formula:**
```
EMI = [P × r × (1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n – 1]
```
Where:
- **P** = Principal loan amount
- **r** = Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12)
- **n** = Number of monthly installments

## Tips for Reducing Your EMI

- Make a **larger down payment** to reduce the principal.
- Choose a **longer tenure** for lower monthly payments (but higher total interest).
- Negotiate a **lower interest rate** with your bank.
- Make **prepayments** or part-payments to reduce the outstanding principal.
